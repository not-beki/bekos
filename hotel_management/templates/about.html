Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1998 as a small 20-room inn, Grand Horizon Hotel began with a simple vision: to create a welcoming retreat for travelers seeking comfort and genuine hospitality.

Over the past two decades, we've grown into a premier luxury destination while maintaining the personal touch that made us special from the beginning.

2005
First major expansion to 100 rooms
2012
Awarded "Best Luxury Hotel" by Hospitality Magazine
2020
Complete renovation with eco-friendly upgrades
